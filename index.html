Helping Flood Affected Families

The Flood Relief System is a web-based application that helps people during flood disasters. It allows affected citizens to report their problems and request help online. The system connects victims with relief authorities and volunteers so that assistance can be provided quickly. It also helps manage rescue operations, distribute food and supplies, and share important safety information. This platform improves communication and coordination, making disaster response faster, organized, and more effective for everyone involved.
